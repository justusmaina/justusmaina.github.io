Who are we?

The Street kids© is a foundation developed in 2018. We are a group of peaple brought together with the purpose of rescuing the young hearts and souls from the streets. Built with compassion, mercy, care and love, we esteem to help the most children get out of the rough conditions they are currently bound in.

There are many children on the streets of town and each and everyday the number increases. The children have many talents and have great potential in class at the same time. if these children could get some little help the situation on our streets would be a whole different story. The children on the street become a major issue in the streets of our city and many of them have extremely rare talents that if explored could result in brilliance.
